Our Story

Infinitie Impex was founded with a passion for exceptional craftsmanship and design. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create premium cabinet hardware that combines functionality with aesthetic excellence.

With decades of experience in metalwork and design, we've established ourselves as a leading provider of high-quality cabinet handles, knobs, pulls, and door hardware. Our products grace homes, hotels, and commercial spaces around the world.

We believe that the details make the difference, and our hardware is designed to be the perfect finishing touch for any space.
